Awaaz, IIT Kharagpur

Student Media Body of IIT Kharagpur

Editor
As a part of the media body of the campus, we analyzed various problems pertaining in the campus and took initiatives to reach out to administration to resolve them. I, along with my batchmates, led a team of 50 members to analyse, develop and publish insights on various topics like internships, placements, student elections, general championship, institute ranking, etc.

My personal motive to join the organisation was to promote constructive journalism . My definition of the term is that any project that we work on as a media body should have one of two motives:
  1. Promulgating important and useful positive information to the students
  2. If we are reporting something negative or something that needs to be improved in the campus, we should ensure that the relevant suggestions reach to the administration and are implemented by them, if feasible.
Things change for the better only when the students' needs reach the administration appropriately through a proper channel, and I believe that a constructive media body provides that channel.
In order to implement the same, I took various personal initiatives, few examples include:
  • Enforcement of Proposal Updates: During student elections, each candidate puts forward a set of proposals that he/she would implement if elected. I led the project of making the elected Student Council accountable to all the students by scrutinizing over and interviewing them for the progress on the proposals
  • Basic Amenities: We conducted a survey among the students to ask them about the various problems that they face regarding basic amenities like canteens, water coolers, street lights, stationery shops etc. in order to find out where these amenities need to be improved in the campus. We reached out to the administration with the results of the survey putting forward the student needs, and appropriate actions were taken by the administration to resolve them.
Apart from this, the team also led various other projects like publication of monthly newsletter, Know Your Professor, Know Your Entrepreneur, Humans of KGP, Jewels of KGP, etc. along with conducting an annual event "Panch Parmeshwar" at Spring Fest.
